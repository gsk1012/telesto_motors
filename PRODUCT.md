# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Dutch car buyers (particulieren and bedrijven) searching for a vehicle. They have a wish list and a budget but lack the time, knowledge, or market access to buy confidently on their own. They want a trusted expert who acts on their behalf from search to delivery.

## Product Purpose
Telesto Motors is an independent Dutch car advisory service. They find, screen, and negotiate a car purchase on behalf of the client, removing stress and guesswork from the process. Success: the client got the right car at a fair price, with no surprises along the way.

## Brand Personality
Premium, trustworthy, personal. Voice: calm confidence. Tone: warm expert who gives straight answers, never sales-y.

## Anti-references
- Generic dealer websites that push volume over relationship
- Corporate finance portals with cold navy-and-gold colour palettes
- SaaS metric dashboards
- Aggressive used-car-lot energy

## Design Principles
1. Client first: every element earns its place by serving the buyer's clarity
2. Warm precision: premium feel, never sterile or transactional
3. Trust through detail: execution quality signals trustworthiness
4. Show, not promise: specific service descriptions over marketing superlatives
5. Dutch directness: helpful and concrete, no filler copy

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA minimum. Reduced motion supported via prefers-reduced-motion media query. Dutch language throughout.
